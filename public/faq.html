Common questions about our services

2. Are revisions always free on my website?

Revisions during your design phase of the website are included with your payment for the website, but after your website is made any further revision is your design or any other functionality of website will have a one time charge fixed cost per revision.

3. Can I contact you for changes within my current website?

Yes, We offer all kinds of web development and management service, we will be happy to handle any request of any kind related to any issues you may have with your websites or want to make a new website, you can always count on us.

A typical website would take around 2 - 3 weeks to make from the idea phase to the final fully developed website. But if you want a fully custom website for your business, the time may vary depending on your project. But you will get regular updates about the progress.

We offer SEO in the Professional and Enterprise plans of ours and we also provide SEO services to any site you may bring us.

7. What does responsiveness means and why it is necessary for my website?

A responsive design simply means a website that has been constructed so that all of the content, images and structure of the site remains the same on any device. Since most of the people are using smaller and portable devices, it is becoming more and more important that your website retains it’s experience on platforms such as mobile. If a user has to take extra effort to view your website on mobile, it is less likely that he would convert to a customer.

Now a days most people tend to research the product they want to buy onine, so having your business online lets people know the services you offer and provide you an edge over other business.
